Disk scheduling simulator

This graph shows different disk scheduling methods on a graph.

You may choose one of the test process lists or enter your own.

Processes are separated by a comma (,), each number indicates necessary sector of the disk.

These can only be positive integers. You may enter up to 10 processes. Disk is limited to 50 sectors.

Read head is at sector 10 in the beginning, ready to move further (towards sectors with larger value).
